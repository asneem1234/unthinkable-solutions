💡 How I Went Beyond the Requirements

1

Smart Document Chunking with Overlap

I didn't just split text randomly. I implemented intelligent segmentation with configurable 1000-char chunks and 200-char overlap to preserve context across boundaries - this ensures no information gets lost between chunks.

2

Dual Search Modes

I built two powerful search options: RAG-powered answers (synthesized by Gemini) AND direct vector search for when you need raw results. Most solutions only offer one approach - I give you both.

3

On-Device Embedding Generation

I integrated Xenova Transformers for local embedding generation - no external API calls needed. This makes my solution faster, more private, and cost-effective compared to cloud-based embedding services.

4

Production Vector Database

I chose Qdrant - an enterprise-grade vector database used by real companies in production, not just in-memory storage. This shows I'm thinking about scalability and real-world deployment.

5

Multi-Format Document Support

I went beyond basic text files and implemented specialized parsers for PDF, DOCX, DOC, and TXT - making this system practical for real-world document management.

6

Real-time Progress Tracking

I added live feedback during document processing with chunk-by-chunk progress updates. Users know exactly what's happening - no black box, no waiting in the dark.

7

Source Attribution & Scoring

Every answer I generate includes source references with relevance scores. This isn't just cool - it's essential for building trust and allowing users to verify AI responses.

8

Serverless Architecture

I optimized everything for Vercel serverless deployment with proper /tmp handling and environment-aware configuration. This is modern, scalable infrastructure - not just localhost code.
